  • To cancel your Netflix subscription:

    1. Open Netflix: Go to the Netflix website or open the Netflix app.
    2. Sign in: Log in to your account.
    3. Go to Account Settings: Click on your profile icon in the top right corner and select Account.
    4. Cancel Membership: Under Membership & Billing, click on Cancel Membership.
    5. Confirm Cancellation: Follow the prompts to confirm the cancellation.

    Your account will remain active until the end of the current billing cycle. You can also restart your subscription at any time.
